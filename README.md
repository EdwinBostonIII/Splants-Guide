## Formatting Rules

### Critical Requirements

✓ **NO em-dashes in body text** (use colons, commas, periods, parentheses instead)
✓ **NO body-text hyphens** except in ranges (4-6 hours), compound words (production-ready), code, URLs, and ASCII diagrams
✓ **Lots of ASCII diagrams** for visual engagement (minimum 1 per major section)
✓ **Visual flourishes throughout** (boxes, separators, progress bars, decision matrices)

### Proper Replacements

- Em-dash (—) → colon (:), comma (,), period (.), or parentheses ()
- Body hyphen ( - ) → comma (,), colon (:), or period (.)

### Examples

❌ Wrong: "The system—which is reliable—processes orders"
✓ Right: "The system (which is reliable) processes orders"

❌ Wrong: "Make.com is easy - just connect modules"
✓ Right: "Make.com is easy, just connect modules"
