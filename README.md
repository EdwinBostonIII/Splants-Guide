## Formatting Rules

### Critical Requirements

‚úì **NO em-dashes in body text** (use colons, commas, periods, parentheses instead)
‚úì **NO body-text hyphens** except in ranges (4-6 hours), compound words (production-ready), code, URLs, and ASCII diagrams
‚úì **Lots of ASCII diagrams** for visual engagement (minimum 1 per major section)
‚úì **Visual flourishes throughout** (boxes, separators, progress bars, decision matrices)

### Proper Replacements

- Em-dash (‚Äî) ‚Üí colon (:), comma (,), period (.), or parentheses ()
- Body hyphen ( - ) ‚Üí comma (,), colon (:), or period (.)

### Examples

‚ùå Wrong: "The system‚Äîwhich is reliable‚Äîprocesses orders"
‚úì Right: "The system (which is reliable) processes orders"

‚ùå Wrong: "Make.com is easy - just connect modules"
‚úì Right: "Make.com is easy, just connect modules"
