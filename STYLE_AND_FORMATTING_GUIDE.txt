PUNCTUATION REPLACEMENTS

Replace em-dashes and body hyphens with:

COLON (:)
  Use for: Introducing lists, examples, or explanations
  Example: "Three steps required: validation, processing, confirmation"

COMMA (,)
  Use for: Separating clauses or adding information
  Example: "The system validates data, then processes the order"

PERIOD (.)
  Use for: Separating independent thoughts into sentences
  Example: "First validate. Then process. Finally confirm."

PARENTHESES ()
  Use for: Clarifying or adding supplementary information
  Example: "The webhook (sent by Stripe) contains order data"

SEMICOLON (;)
  Use for: Connecting related independent clauses
  Example: "Printful is fastest; Printify is cheapest; Gooten is backup"
